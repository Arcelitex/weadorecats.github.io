Essential Care Tips

01

Fresh Water Every Single Day

Cats are notoriously bad at drinking enough water. Change their bowl daily — or consider a cat water fountain, which many cats prefer because running water feels instinctively safer to them.
02

The "One Litter Box Per Cat + One Extra" Rule

This is the golden rule most cat owners skip. If you have 1 cat, you need 2 litter boxes. Skipping this causes stress, territorial issues, and accidents outside the box.
03

Play for at Least 15 Minutes Daily

Indoor cats need stimulation. A bored cat becomes a destructive cat. Fifteen minutes of wand-toy play burns energy, keeps weight healthy, and deepens your bond.
04

Annual Vet Checkups (Even for Healthy Cats)

Cats hide illness incredibly well. By the time symptoms are visible, a condition is often advanced. Annual bloodwork catches kidney disease, thyroid issues, and diabetes early — when they're treatable.
05

Brush Your Cat 2–3x Per Week

Reduces hairballs, keeps their coat shiny, and gives you time to spot lumps, fleas, or skin issues early. Most cats grow to love brushing once they're used to it.
